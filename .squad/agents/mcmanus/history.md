- **UI Scale:** 28 content pages + 5 redirects, heavy component usage (Navbar, RegimeBadge, charts, tables, data viz)
- **Key pages:** /dashboard (Command Centre), /scan (7-Stage Scanner), /plan (Weekly Execution Board), /portfolio/positions, /risk, /settings, /trade-log, /backtest, /signal-audit, /causal-audit, /execution-quality
